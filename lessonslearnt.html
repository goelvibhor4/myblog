Lessons learned in 3 years of my college life

Yes, I also learnt the hard way.

Posted by Vibhor Goel on June 28,2015

College is a period where we never quit finding out about ourselves and never stop growing as an individual. I believe that there is more to the college experience than sitting in libraries and focusing purely on your CGPA. College is an amazing experience, where you meet new people, find interesting new things to do, and learn a lot about yourself.

I've also had some incredible life experiences.None of the lessons I learned came easily. Here are just a few of the numerous lessons I learned in three years of my college life:

1.You are surrounded by cool individuals, and they are all really near your own particular age.

1.Learn how to manage stress.

At whatever point I had a feeling that everything was turning out to be a lot for me to handle I used to put the outside world on hold for a few days. I truly believe despite what you do, give yourself some time every once in a while to temporarily forget about all of life's responsibilities and enjoy your own company.

2.You can’t please everyone.

Some people will always tell you what you did wrong, and then hesitate to compliment you for what you did right. Don’t be one of them, and don’t put up with them.Simply walk away because any bit of time you spend on these people will be wasted.

3.There’s no such thing as destiny.

I would really put it simple “Work hard and get rewards for them”.Believing good days will come and sitting and doing nothing will certainly not help . Your main motive should be doing good. we should stop blaming other and we must believe that "everything is our own responsibility

Anyway after having a decent involvement in this field I would recommend you one thing, this isn't the way for everyone, and there are drawbacks. You might feel “I don’t have enough time in the day” issue. I thought I had little time for myself before working for startups. Presently I recognize what occupied is. Also,In college, individuals are continually asking you “what do you need do after you graduate?” and debating over this question for hours. Then my simple answer to them is “Do what you Love !!”. Just follow your dreams.
